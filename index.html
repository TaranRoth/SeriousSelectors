Rules of checkers:

  1. You and your opponent take turns moving.
  2. On your move, you can move one of your checkers forward diagonally or capture a checker if you are next to it by "jumping" over that checker.
  3. You can't capture a checker if the spot you would move to after taking that checker is occupied.
  4. If you can capture another checker right after capturing, you can do so on the same turn.
  5. If your checker reaches the final row on your opponent's side of the board, that checker can now move and capture in all four diagonal directions.
